Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

EarningsBloom ("we", "us", or "our") operates earningsbloom.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Give Us

If you contact us via email or our contact form, we collect your name and email address solely to respond to your message.

Information Collected Automatically

Like most websites, we automatically collect certain technical information when you visit:

  • Log data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent, referring URL.
  • Cookies: Small text files stored on your device. See the Cookies section below.

2. Cookies & Advertising

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Google AdSense: We serve advertisements through Google AdSense. Google uses cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.
  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymised traffic data. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Functional cookies: Essential cookies that ensure the website works correctly.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the EarningsBloom website
  • To respond to support queries
  • To understand site usage and improve user experience
  • To serve relevant advertisements via Google AdSense

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data independently:

  • Google AdSense — advertising network (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Google Analytics — website analytics
  • Supabase — database infrastructure
  • Google Cloud Run — hosting infrastructure

EarningsBloom does not sell your personal data to any third party.

5. Data Retention

Contact form submissions and emails are retained for up to 12 months. Anonymised analytics data may be retained longer per Google Analytics policy.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of personalised advertising (see Cookie section above)

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@earningsbloom.com.

7. Children's Privacy

EarningsBloom is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at privacy@earningsbloom.com.
