About EarningsBloom

We believe every investor — regardless of whether they have a Bloomberg Terminal — deserves instant, clear, unbiased summaries of earnings calls.

👤 About the Founder
👨‍💻
Abhi
Software Engineer & AI Builder · EarningsBloom Founder & Editor

Abhi is a software engineer and AI builder who created EarningsBloom to make earnings call insights accessible to every investor — not just those with expensive financial terminals. He designs, maintains, and reviews all AI pipelines on the platform, combining technical expertise with a passion for financial literacy.

🤔 The Problem We Solve
Every quarter, public companies hold earnings calls that produce 80–120 page transcripts. Reading them is impractical. News articles summarizing them are often biased, incomplete, or paywalled. Bloomberg and Seeking Alpha charge hundreds per month for proper summaries. We changed that.
⚙️ How It Works
Our pipeline monitors the SEC EDGAR database automatically during earnings seasons (January, April, July, October). When a new transcript is filed, we download it, clean it, and send it through a two-stage AI pipeline — reviewed by Abhi for quality:
  • Stage 1: Extract key financial numbers (revenue, EPS, beat/miss)
  • Stage 2: Generate human-quality insights — TLDR, wins, concerns, CEO guidance, key quote, and overall sentiment
The result is published to a permanent, indexed URL within minutes of the filing.
✅ Our Editorial Standards
  • Source: All data is sourced directly from official SEC EDGAR filings — the same source professional analysts use.
  • Accuracy: Key financial numbers (Revenue, EPS) are extracted programmatically and cross-checked against the original filing.
  • Transparency: Every summary links directly to the original SEC filing so readers can verify independently.
  • Limitations: AI summaries can occasionally misinterpret nuance. Always verify key numbers with official company reports.
⚠️ Important Disclaimer
EarningsBloom summaries are AI-assisted and are provided for informational purposes only.
