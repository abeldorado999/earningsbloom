About EarningsBloom

We believe every investor — regardless of whether they have a Bloomberg Terminal — deserves instant, clear, unbiased summaries of earnings calls.

🤔 The Problem We Solve
Every quarter, public companies hold earnings calls that produce 80–120 page transcripts. Reading them is impractical. News articles summarizing them are often biased, incomplete, or paywalled. Bloomberg and Seeking Alpha charge hundreds per month for proper summaries. We changed that.
⚙️ How It Works
Our automated pipeline monitors the SEC EDGAR database every 30 minutes during earnings season. When a new transcript is filed, we download it, clean it, and send it through a two-stage Google Gemini AI pipeline:
  • Stage 1: Extract key financial numbers (revenue, EPS, beat/miss)
  • Stage 2: Generate human-quality insights — TLDR, wins, concerns, CEO guidance, key quote, and overall sentiment
The result is published to a permanent, indexed URL within minutes of the filing.
⚠️ Important Disclaimer
EarningsBloom summaries are generated by AI and are provided for informational purposes only. They do NOT constitute financial advice, investment recommendations, or solicitations to buy or sell any security. Always conduct your own research and consult a qualified financial advisor before making investment decisions. AI-generated content may contain errors — always verify key numbers with official filings.
